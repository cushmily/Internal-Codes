Welcome to Internal Codes.

If you like the board game Codenames, you may enjoy this with the same mechainics!

Internal Codes is a utility of the famous party game called Codesnames, you can use it to start your Codenames gaming with your friend anywhere easily.

In Codenames, players are separated into two teams to compete to see who can make contact with all of their agents first. Leaders give one-word clues that can point to multiple words on the board. Their teammates try to guess words of the right color while avoiding those that belong to the opposing team. And everyone wants to avoid the assassin.
